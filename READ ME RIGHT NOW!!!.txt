IMPORTANT
- The mod LTV_Economy needs WrapUI to function properly. Please add both of these to your mods folder
- Certain aspects of the mod can be modified under LTV_Economy/data/config/ltvEcon/
- The dynamic configs generated for industries under saves/common/dynamic_industry_config.json.data might be outdated, in which case it should be deleted so a fresh one can be created. Note that adding a new mod that adds industries will re-create the config.
